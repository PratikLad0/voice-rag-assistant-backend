Cars Sample Dataset
====================
These documents are intended for a customer-care Voice RAG demo in the automotive domain.
- Mix of Markdown, TXT, and a JSON FAQ compatible with the provided `ingest.py`.
- Titles appear in citations (e.g., "(from Warranty Policy)").

Suggested questions
- What's your returns policy?
- How long is the EV battery warranty?
- Can I exchange my car for another model?
- How do I cancel my order?
- What's included in roadside assistance?
